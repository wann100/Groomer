Welcome to HeadWay! The ultimate multi platform application, that helps people find a new look look or create one. We are dedicated to keeping this application simple and free. HeadWay is completely free of charge and has a (WePay)payment system which Hair Stylist, or Hair Dressers may use process payments. More details on the payment system can be found at www.wepay.com.

How to sign up?

In order to register for HeadWay, just go hit enter or any register button that is available. Once you have interact with any of those buttons you will be given, two options.

-Options-

Register as an User:

In order to leave comments and make appointments with barbers, you must register and confirm your account via email

Register as a Hair Dresser:

Once you chose this option, you can create a hair dresser account. This allows you to add services, add a payment system, and get and confirm appointments. You can also upload 1 photo that best represents you
